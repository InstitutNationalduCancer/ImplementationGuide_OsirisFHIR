Resource Profile: Consent for Genetic Data Usage

Official URL: https://ig-osiris.cancer.fr/ig/osiris/StructureDefinition/genetic-consent Version: 1.1.0
Draft as of 2025-04-22 Computable Name: GeneticConsent

The genetic consent resource indicates if a patient has given his authorization to share and use its genetics data.

Usage:

  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Consent/fhir-osiris-example-consent-1 and Consent/fhir-osiris-example-consent-2
