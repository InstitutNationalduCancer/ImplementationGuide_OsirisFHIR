Resource Profile: Treatment

Official URL: https://ig-osiris.cancer.fr/ig/osiris/StructureDefinition/treatment Version: 1.1.0
Draft as of 2025-04-22 Computable Name: Treatment

Description of a treatment plan related to an Oncology Patient.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile: Oncology-related Medication Statement, Oncology Surgery, Radiotherapy course Summary and Response Evaluation
  • Examples for this Resource Profile: CarePlan/fhir-osiris-example-treatment-1, CarePlan/fhir-osiris-example-treatment-2 and CarePlan/fhir-osiris-example-treatment-radiotherapy
