Resource Profile: Cancer Organization

Official URL: https://build.fhir.org/ig/arkhn/arkhn-ig-osiris/StructureDefinition/onco-organization Version: 2.1.0
Draft as of 2024-05-15 Computable Name: OncoOrganization

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile: Oncology Patient and Patient Origin Center
