Resource Profile: Biological Sample

Official URL: https://build.fhir.org/ig/arkhn/arkhn-ig-osiris/StructureDefinition/onco-biological-sample Version: 2.1.0
Draft as of 2024-05-13 Computable Name: BiologicalSample
