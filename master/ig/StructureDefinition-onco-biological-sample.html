Resource Profile: Biological Sample

Official URL: https://ig-osiris.cancer.fr/ig/osiris/StructureDefinition/onco-biological-sample Version: 1.1.0
Draft as of 2025-04-22 Computable Name: BiologicalSample

Description of a biological sample coming from an oncology Patient, with its storage condition.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile: Biological Sample and Sequencing Analysis
  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Specimen/fhir-osiris-example-biologicalsample-1 and Specimen/fhir-osiris-example-biologicalsample-2
