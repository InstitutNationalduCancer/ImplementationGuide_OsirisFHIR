Resource Profile: Radiotherapy Volume Profile

Official URL: https://ig-osiris.cancer.fr/ig/osiris/StructureDefinition/radiotherapy-volume Version: 1.1.0
Draft as of 2025-04-22 Computable Name: RadiotherapyVolume

A volume of the body treated by radiotherapy (plannes or delivered).
