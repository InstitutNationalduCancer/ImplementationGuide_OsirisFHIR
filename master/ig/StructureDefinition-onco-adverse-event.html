Resource Profile: Oncologic Adverse Event

Official URL: https://build.fhir.org/ig/arkhn/arkhn-ig-osiris/StructureDefinition/onco-adverse-event Version: 2.1.0
Draft as of 2024-05-15 Computable Name: OncoAdverseEvent

Adverse event related to a cancer-related treatment associated to a grade from MEDDRA terminology (ex: 10047700: “Vomiting”).

Usage:

  • Examples for this Resource Profile: AdverseEvent/fhir-osiris-example-adverseevent-1
