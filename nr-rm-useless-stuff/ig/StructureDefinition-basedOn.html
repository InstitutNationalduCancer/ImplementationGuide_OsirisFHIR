Summary

Complex Extension: Connection with the imaging module (ImagingStudy and ROI Segmentation)

  • imagingStudyRef: Reference: Imaging Study to which the reference series (UID series) belong
  • seriesUID: string: Series Instance UID of the planning image. Dicom Tag (0020,000E)
  • rtStructUID: Reference: SOP reference Instance UID of RT Structure Set. Dicom Tag (0008,1155)
